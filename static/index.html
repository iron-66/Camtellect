Problem Statement

The interface is the bottleneck

Today, LLMs are powerful - but the way we interact with them is clumsy. To access this intelligence, we pull out a phone, unlock it, open an app, and type with our fingertips. This workflow feels natural only because we’ve adapted to it, not because it’s efficient.

From an evolutionary lens, tapping glass screens with our thumbs is a poor match for how humans actually think and communicate. In neurobiology, there’s a simple rule: behavior follows energy. The more effort a task requires, the less often we do it.

Right now, the energy cost of engaging with LLMs is too high - every interaction demands extra steps, breaking the natural flow of thought. This friction keeps most people from using external intelligence as often as they could.

We see a different path. To unlock the true potential of LLMs, the interface must disappear into daily life. Our mission is to reduce the cognitive and physical energy cost of interaction - making it native, frictionless, and LLM-first.

The Concept of Augmented Intelligence

Extend your mind, don’t replace it

Augmented Intelligence is not about replacing the human mind. It’s about extending it - giving every person a seamless cognitive layer that works in real time, always on, always accessible.

Just as eyeglasses expand vision and electricity expands power, Augmented Intelligence expands thought. It takes the friction out of accessing knowledge and transforms LLMs from tools you “open” into a natural part of how you live, act, and decide.
